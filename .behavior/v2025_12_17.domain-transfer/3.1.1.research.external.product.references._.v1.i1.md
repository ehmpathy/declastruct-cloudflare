## category.5: registrar terminology

### primary references

| ref | url | purpose |
|-----|-----|---------|
| [13] | https://www.godaddy.com/help/what-is-the-difference-between-a-registry-registrar-and-registrant-8039 | registry/registrar/registrant |

### key concepts established

- registry: "manages top-level domain names... operates the master database for specific top-level domains" [13]
- registrar: "an accredited organization... that sells domain names to the public" [13]
- registrant: "the person or company who registers a domain name" [13]
- analogy: "The registry is the publisher, the registrar is the bookstore, and the registrant is the buyer" [13]
