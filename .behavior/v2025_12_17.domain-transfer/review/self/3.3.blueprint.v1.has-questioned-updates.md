## why updates are required

criteria usecase.3 states:
> given multiple A records for load distribution are declared
>   when setDomainDnsRecord is called for each
>     then all records are created

current unique key `['zone', 'name', 'type']` would cause collision for:
- A record 1: zone=example.com, name=api, type=A, content=192.168.1.1
- A record 2: zone=example.com, name=api, type=A, content=192.168.1.2

fix: add `content` to unique key enables multiple A records per (zone, name, type).
